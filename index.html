Australia's IT Career Landscape

This visual story explores what the Australian IT job market looks like today. It focuses on salary, job roles, and language backgrounds in the industry so that everyday viewers can better understand who works in IT, where opportunities may be, and how the field is changing.

Age group and average salary

This chart introduces the economic side of the IT industry. It helps viewers compare how earnings change across life stages. That makes the topic personally relevant for students, career changers, and working adults.

Language background in IT

IT is not only about jobs and salary. This chart adds a social dimension by showing who participates in the field. It helps the audience think about representation, inclusion, and how diverse the workforce may be.

Distribution of IT jobs

This section shifts the focus from people to occupations. It shows which roles appear most often and helps viewers understand that the IT industry includes many different career paths rather than one single kind of job.

Industry In IT

This chart explores which IT industries employ the most people. It helps viewers understand where opportunities are concentrated across the Australian technology sector.
